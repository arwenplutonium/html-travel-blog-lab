Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island National Park

Beautiful and mysterious, the jungles of Brazil are home to many wonders of the world and miracles of nature. Fire Island National Park in particular is home to the infamous boiling river of Brazil. A natural wonder made possible by the plethora of underwater geysers that consistently raise the temperature of the river. The water is hot enough to boil an entire egg in! Can you believe it? The natural reserve is heavily protected by the government of the country and rightfully so. My experience was absolutely one to write home about. When we visited the boiling river, our tour guide was careful to warn us of its extremely high temperatures and I was careful enough not to listen. This unfortunately resulted in sustaining a particularly nasty burn from the river's very hot water. Luckily enough the wound was treated with the urgency required and ended up leaving little more than a very prolific scar. All in all the trip truly was magical and amazing to behold as the national park still had a plethora of wonders to showcase such as the ever running waterfalls, ever so elegant. How wonderful the Brazilian natural world is! Truly nature is something to behold.
